About Jomovate

We work in the gap between idea and outcome

Jomovate started from a simple observation: most organizations don't lack good ideas, they lack the hands-on capacity to carry one from a whiteboard to something customers actually use. We built the firm to be that capacity — for enterprises, small businesses and the people they serve.

How we operate
Innovate
Small teams, close to the work

We staff engagements with people who build, not just advise — strategists sit next to the people writing code and running campaigns.

Grow
Measured against outcomes

Every engagement is scoped against a business result, not a deliverable — so success looks the same to us as it does to you.

Elevate
Built to hand off

We document and train as we go, so the work is still improving the business long after our part of it ends.

Meet the Founder

Daramola Joseph Omoyele

Jomovate was founded by Daramola Joseph Omoyele, a data analyst, researcher and digital product developer with experience in statistical analysis, business intelligence, automation and service improvement.

His work focuses on transforming complex operational challenges into practical digital tools that improve communication, performance and decision-making.
